EPFL Racing Team

EPFL Racing Team is a student-led engineering project developing a fully electric and autonomous Formula Student race car, combining design, manufacturing and competition in a high-performance environment.

From technical contribution to team leadership

During my first year, I designed and produced the full electrical integration kit using composite, aluminium and 3D-printed materials, creating a lightweight, rigid and waterproof solution. I also contributed to the manufacturing of major composite parts, including the monocoque.

In my second year, as Head of Monocoque and Layup Manufacturing, I led the production of the carbon monocoque and improved the electronic integration approach, while also developing validated inserts for composite and aluminium parts.
